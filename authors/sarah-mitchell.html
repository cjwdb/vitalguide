Sarah Mitchell

MS, RDN — Registered Dietitian Nutritionist

Sarah Mitchell is a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN) with a Master's degree in Nutritional Sciences and over a decade of experience evaluating clinical nutrition research. She leads VitalGuide's editorial review process, ensuring every product recommendation and supplement review reflects current scientific evidence and best practices in nutrition. Sarah specialises in translating complex research on micronutrients, adaptogens, and ergogenic aids into clear, actionable guidance for health-conscious consumers. Her work is grounded in peer-reviewed literature, and she applies a rigorous, evidence-first standard to every category she covers.

Editorial Standards

Sarah applies a rigorous evidence-first review process to all content at VitalGuide. Every supplement recommendation is grounded in peer-reviewed clinical trials, with clear disclosure of the strength of available evidence. She prioritises human clinical data over animal studies or in vitro research, and is transparent about gaps in the research.
