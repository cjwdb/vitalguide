How We Review

Our methodology for independent, trustworthy product evaluations

Our Review Philosophy

Every product on VitalGuide goes through a structured evaluation process. We prioritize evidence over hype — our goal is to surface the products most likely to make a real difference for real people, not the ones with the biggest marketing budgets.

What We Evaluate

1. Ingredient Quality & Formulation (Supplements)

For supplements, we assess:

  • Active ingredient dosages versus research-backed effective doses
  • Form of ingredients (e.g., magnesium glycinate vs. magnesium oxide)
  • Third-party testing certifications (USP, NSF, Informed Sport)
  • Presence of unnecessary fillers, allergens, or proprietary blends

2. User Reviews & Real-World Feedback

We analyze thousands of verified Amazon reviews to surface patterns in real-world usage — separating genuine feedback from marketing noise. We look at:

  • Overall rating distribution (not just the average)
  • Common complaints and praise across verified purchasers
  • Long-term use feedback versus initial impressions

3. Value for Money

We calculate cost per serving / cost per use and compare it to similar products in the category. A higher-priced product may still be the best value if the ingredient quality justifies it.

4. Brand Transparency

We favor brands that clearly disclose:

  • Full ingredient labels with no proprietary blends for key actives
  • Manufacturing location and certifications
  • Clear return and satisfaction policies

5. Scientific Evidence

For health claims, we reference peer-reviewed research from PubMed and other credible sources. We note when claims are well-supported by science, emerging, or lacking strong evidence.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not accept paid placements or "sponsored" rankings
  • We do not change reviews based on affiliate commission rates
  • We do not accept free products from manufacturers in exchange for favorable coverage

Updating Our Reviews

Health research evolves and products change formulations. We review and update our content regularly to ensure recommendations remain accurate and current.
